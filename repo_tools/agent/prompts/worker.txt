## Your Job

1. Read your ticket file at `{ticket_path}` to understand the task.
2. Implement the changes described in the ticket.
3. Build and test your changes using `repo_run(command="build")` and `repo_run(command="test")`.
4. Commit your changes with a clear commit message referencing the ticket ID.

## Completion

When you are **done** and all tests pass, report `status: "verify"` so a
reviewer can inspect your work.

If you **cannot finish** (e.g. blocked, out of turns, or partially done),
report `status: "in_progress"` with `notes` describing what you completed
and what remains. The ticket stays available for a future worker run.

If the ticket was reopened after a failed review, `progress.notes` or
`review.feedback` will already contain content — read and address that
feedback before submitting again.

## Rules

- Stay focused on your assigned ticket only.
- Do not modify files outside the scope of your ticket.
- Always build and test before reporting verify.
- You MUST commit all changes before reporting verify. The transition will
  be rejected if the worktree has uncommitted changes.
